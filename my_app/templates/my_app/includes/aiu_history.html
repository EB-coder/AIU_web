About AIU

Ala-Too University

is one of the leading international universities in Central Asia. The university was founded in 1996 by the International Educational Institution "Sapat." IAU is a legal entity, a private institution that operates in accordance with the legislation of the Kyrgyz Republic. In 2013, IAU opened the IT and Business College, followed by the Distance Learning Center, master's and doctoral programs, which expanded the prospects for new growth and development of the university. Currently, IAU has 4 academic faculties, 19 undergraduate programs, 2 undergraduate distance learning programs, 2 master's distance learning programs, 10 master's programs, 6 doctoral programs, 3 Ph.D. programs, and 3 institutes.
