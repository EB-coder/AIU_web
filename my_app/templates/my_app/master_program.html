Information Section

For specialists in the field of information technology who want to engage in scientific activities
from 2022 there is an opportunity to enroll in basic doctoral studies for a doctorate degree in the PHD profile.,
Basic doctoral studies combine the basic competencies of a master's or specialist degree, allowing the development of
highly specialized scientific areas under the guidance of a scientific supervisor on a fixed topic.
The topic of the dissertation must be relevant, meet modern requirements and benefit the country.
The curricula of basic doctoral studies include scientific seminars, research conferences, round tables
with the participation of leading experts, as well as master classes on writing scientific articles.
Language of instruction: English
Deadlines for submitting documents: August 31 - November 1 on weekdays from 10:00 to 17:00
